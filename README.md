## 🧠 About Me

I'm an **Artificial Intelligence student** at **Pak-Austria Fachhochschule Institute of Applied Sciences and Technology**, currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree while actively building hands-on experience across AI, automation, and software development.

I currently serve as an **AI Intern at M-Tech**, where I'm gaining practical exposure to AI technologies, applied machine learning workflows, and real-world software development practices. Alongside this, I work as a **GTM Growth Associate**, where I bridge technical understanding with go-to-market strategy and growth execution. I also build **AI-powered N8n chatbots**, automating workflows and conversational systems for real business use cases.

I enjoy building **AI-powered solutions, automation tools, and intelligent software systems** — and I believe that continuous learning, practical implementation, and innovation are the foundation of becoming an exceptional AI engineer.
